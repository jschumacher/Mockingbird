Mockingbird

Our Vision is a world in which every child attains the right to food, safety, education and play. We hope that by doing our little bit, we can help make that world.

We're dedicated to helping impoverished kids experience what all kids deserve – the freedom of having a childhood...

We believe that this means the small things – having a book, a toy or a good meal – something to call their own, something to help them stop worrying about surviving... and start having a bit of joy!

We draw from the UN Convention of the Rights of the Child in our belief that all children have the same right to develop their potential – all children, in all situations, all of the time, everywhere.

... So we ask you to come join us and help make that happen!
